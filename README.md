# University Course Reality Check Platform

## Overview

Official university course descriptions often fail to reflect the real student experience.  
This platform allows students to **anonymously share honest feedback** about university courses â€” including workload grading fairness, exam difficulty, and attendance strictness.
Future students can make **informed decisions** instead of choosing courses blindly.

---

## Core Features

- Anonymous course reviews to encourage honest feedback
- Course ratings for:
  - Workload
  - Exam difficulty
  - Grading fairness
  - Attendance strictness
- Written student experiences including:
  - Pros & cons
  - Tips and advice
- Community voting to highlight the most helpful and accurate reviews
- Course search and filtering by:
  - University
  - Department
  - Difficulty level
- Moderation and reporting system to prevent abuse and false reviews
